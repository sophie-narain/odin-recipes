Pasta Sauce

Description

Pasta sauce with tomatoes, garlic and herbs

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Half an onion
  • Garlic clove
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Dried basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the garlic and onion in the olive oil
  2. Stir in the other ingredients and simmer for 1 hour
