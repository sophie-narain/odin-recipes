Potato Wedges

Description

Oven baked potato wedges

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees c
  2. Slice potatoes and spread on a baking tray. Add olive oil, salt and pepper
  3. Bake for 30 minutes
